_In a world full sorrows_  
_and everlasting despair_  
_I am worried less about tomorrow_  
_fighting a fraction of today's fear_  

_motionless are eyes of the dead_  
_emotionless are the faces living here_  
_with a scar the feudals tread_  
_whom we can't even see let alone, bear_  

_stay with me, stay here my soul_  
_the morning will end the mourning_  
_shall you know a little truth that_  
_escape you yearn only leads to anew scorning_
